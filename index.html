Humans of Muse

How it works

From stream
to portfolio.

Four steps between you and a virtual portfolio of artist "stocks." No sign-up, no broker, no real money at risk.

i

Discover

Browse 105 hand-picked artists across pop, hip-hop, indie, and more. Each profile shows Spotify and YouTube stats and a live price.

ii

Invest

Buy shares with a market order. Every new player starts with €100,000 in virtual credits. No deposit, no KYC, no account.

iii

Track

Prices refresh every 30 minutes, 24/7, from real Spotify monthly listeners and YouTube channel stats — plus chart and radio boosts.

iv

Compare

Sell anytime to lock in a virtual gain or cut a virtual loss. See if you can actually spot a rising artist before the charts do.
