Under the hood

The pricing engine.

Every artist's price comes from a simple, readable formula. No black box. Here's exactly how the number on screen is built.

01

Raw ingest

A cron job pulls Spotify monthly listeners and YouTube channel stats (subscribers, total views) for every listed artist every 30 minutes, 24/7. That's the entire feed today.

02

Base formula

Price per share = monthly listeners × €0.03 per 1,000,000. So an artist with 30M monthly listeners sits around €0.90 before boosts. Bigger audience = higher price.

03

Boost multipliers

YouTube scale adds a small premium. Chart position on major streaming charts and recent radio airplay add bounded boosts on top. All multipliers are capped so a single viral moment can't lift a price by 10x overnight.

04

Smoothing

Raw values go through an exponential moving average so the price doesn't whiplash on every data refresh. Over time, an artist's trajectory shows up cleanly instead of as noise.
