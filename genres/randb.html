How R&B prices work on Muses

Every artist in the R&B sector trades as a "stock" with a price driven by three real signals:

  • Spotify monthly listeners — the largest weight. Rising listeners means rising market share.
  • YouTube channel reach — total views plus 30-day trend.
  • Chart positions and cultural momentum — Billboard data, viral moments, release timing.

Trading activity moves the price within a ±25% band around the streaming-derived "fair value", so demand can lift an artist above their fundamentals — and selling pressure can push them below.
