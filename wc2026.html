How it works

Predict the score of every World Cup match before kickoff. Points are awarded automatically when matches finish — no admin, no spreadsheets.

Scoring

  • 5 points — exact score (you said 2–1, it ended 2–1)
  • 3 points — right winner and right goal difference (you said 2–1, it ended 3–2) — or a correctly predicted draw with the wrong score
  • 2 points — right winner only
  • 0 points — wrong result
  • +15 points — your champion pick wins the World Cup (Bonus tab)
  • +10 points — your top-scorer pick wins the Golden Boot (Bonus tab; a tie for most goals counts, own goals and shootout kicks don't)
  • +3 points — knockout matches only: correctly predict how it's decided — 90 minutes, extra time, or penalties
  • +1 point — if you predicted penalties and also correctly pick which team wins the shootout

The fine print

  • Predictions save automatically and lock 30 minutes before kickoff (enforced server-side). Until then, use Edit to change them as often as you like.
  • Other people's predictions stay hidden until the lock (30 min before kickoff). After that they show on the match card — and you can tap any player on the leaderboard to see everything they filled in.
  • Knockout matches count the score after extra time; penalty shootouts don't add goals. Predicting a draw in a knockout match is allowed. On top of the score you predict how it's decided (90 min / extra time / penalties) for +3.
  • Bonus picks (champion + top scorer) stay open until the deadline shown on the Bonus tab.
  • Tiebreakers: most exact scores, then most correct results.
  • Results come from ESPN automatically: live scores roughly every minute while you have the page open, final results within ~30 minutes.
