How it works

Predict the score of every World Cup match before kickoff. Points are awarded automatically when matches finish — no admin, no spreadsheets.

Scoring

  • 5 points — exact score (you said 2–1, it ended 2–1)
  • 3 points — right winner and right goal difference (you said 2–1, it ended 3–2) — or a correctly predicted draw with the wrong score
  • 2 points — right winner only
  • 0 points — wrong result
  • +15 points — your champion pick wins the World Cup

The fine print

  • Predictions lock 30 minutes before kickoff (enforced server-side). Until then you can add or change them as often as you like.
  • Other people's predictions stay hidden until the lock (30 min before kickoff), then everyone's picks are revealed on the match card.
  • Knockout matches count the score after extra time; penalty shootouts don't add goals. Predicting a draw in a knockout match is allowed.
  • Champion pick + total-goals guess lock 30 minutes before the opening match.
  • Tiebreakers: most exact scores, then whoever is closest to the total goals scored in the tournament.
  • Results come from ESPN automatically: live scores roughly every minute while you have the page open, final results within ~30 minutes.
